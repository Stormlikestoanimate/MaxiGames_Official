Duck Life

Description

Duck Life (also known as Duck Life: Origin in the Retro Pack or Duck Life 1) is the first installment in the Duck Life series, released on February 28th, 2010. In this game, the player trains a duck (later revealed to be Dory) to win races in order to raise enough money to save the farm.

Description by Duck Life Wiki

Controls

Use mouse to interact with UI

Use Up and Down arrow keys to Jump and Dive. And Left and Right to move duck.
